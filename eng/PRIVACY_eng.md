# Loragica Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 19, 2026
**Document version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Introduction

Loragica ("we", "us", or "our") values the privacy of everyone who visits our website at [loragica.com](https://loragica.com) / [loragica.id](https://loragica.id) ("Site"). This document explains what data we may collect, what it is used for, and the rights you have regarding that data.

**Important to understand from the start:** Loragica is currently in the **branding and early validation stage**. This Site is an introductory brand page — there is no user account system (login/registration) yet, no payments, and no storage of personal learning data. This policy will be updated as soon as those features are released, and we will clearly indicate when significant updates occur.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data you provide directly
If the Site provides any forms (such as contact forms, interest/waitlist registrations, or future paid cohort registrations), we only collect data that you enter voluntarily, such as:
* Name
* Email address
* Messages or inquiries you send

### 2.2 Automatic technical data
Because the Site is hosted on **Cloudflare Pages**, certain basic technical data (such as IP address, browser type, pages accessed, and time of visit) may be recorded automatically through Cloudflare's infrastructure for security and network performance purposes. We do not run third-party tracking analytics systems (such as Google Analytics) ourselves unless stated otherwise in section 2.3 below.

### 2.3 Cookies
This Site currently **does not use tracking cookies** for advertising or profiling. If we add analytics tools that use cookies in the future, we will update this section and provide an appropriate consent mechanism.

---

## 3. How We Use Data

The data you provide directly (see 2.1) is only used to:
* Reply to inquiries or messages you send
* Contact you regarding programs you have registered interest in (e.g., learning cohorts)
* Improve and develop the Site and Loragica's services

We **do not sell, rent, or trade** personal data to any third party.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

The form data you submit is stored using trusted third-party services (such as email or form-handling providers connected to the Site). We strive to use services with reasonable security standards, but like any internet service, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Third Parties

This Site is hosted using **Cloudflare Pages**. Use of this Site is indirectly subject to the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for matters relating to their network infrastructure.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties outside of those mentioned in this document, unless required by applicable law in Indonesia.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
* Request a copy of the personal data we store about you
* Request us to update or delete that data
* Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribing from the email list)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information in section 8.
